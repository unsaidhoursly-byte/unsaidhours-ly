You're Not Alone — Help Exists

Whether you need someone to talk to right now, or just want to take better care of yourself — these resources are for you.

⚠️ If You're in Crisis, Seek Immediate Help

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or having thoughts of self-harm, please reach out to one of the helplines below or go to your nearest emergency room. You matter. You deserve help.

Self-Care Tips 🌿

Small things that can make a real difference on hard days.

🌬️ Breathe

Try the 4-7-8 technique: inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale for 8. It calms your nervous system in minutes.

💧 Hydrate

Dehydration worsens anxiety and fatigue. A glass of water won't fix everything — but it's a start.

📵 Unplug

Put your phone away for 30 minutes. The world can wait. You need this time for yourself.

🚶 Move

Even a 10-minute walk can shift your mood. Movement isn't about fitness — it's about feeling alive.

☀️ Sunlight

Five minutes in natural light boosts serotonin. Open a window, step outside, feel the sun.

🎵 Music

Create a "safe" playlist. Songs that ground you, calm you, or just let you feel what you need to feel.

Journaling Prompts ✍️

When you can't say it out loud, try writing it down. Start with one of these.

📖

Today I feel...

Don't filter it. Don't judge it. Just write the first emotion that comes to mind and let it flow.

💫

One thing I'm proud of today

Even if it's getting out of bed. Even if it's reading this page. That counts.

💌

A letter to my younger self

What would you say? What do you wish someone had told you when you needed it most?

🌱

If healing had a shape, it would look like...

Let your mind wander. There are no wrong answers in a journal that's just for you.
